Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a pandit?

You can book a pandit through our website or mobile app. Simply select your service, choose a date and time, and complete the booking process. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services including wedding ceremonies, house warming ceremonies, naming ceremonies, and various religious rituals. Each service can be customized according to your needs.

How much does it cost to book a pandit?

Our pricing varies based on the type of ceremony, duration, and the experience level of the pandit. You can find detailed pricing information on our services page or by contacting our support team.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on the timing of your cancellation.

How do I know if a pandit is available?

Our booking system shows real-time availability of pandits. You can check their schedules and book them directly through our platform. You can also contact our support team for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. All payments are secure and encrypted.
